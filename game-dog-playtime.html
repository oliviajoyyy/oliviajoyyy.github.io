Dog Playtime

This is a game where you can use different items to play with the dog. It's intended to be played for fun without a way to "win" or "lose", but to make the game more interesting, there is a small challenge in finding which items the dog will play with in each level. There's 5 items you can choose from in the selection bar. You can also restart anytime by clicking the restart button.
