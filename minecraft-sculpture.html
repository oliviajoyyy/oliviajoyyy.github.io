Marvel Minecraft Sculpture

The objective of this project was to use a virtual environment to design a sculpture representing an object of cultural significance. The sculpture was also created with the idea that it would be 3D printed in the next project.

Iron Man Helmet

I created a sculpture of the Iron Man helmet because it represents Marvel, which I am a big fan of. It is culturally significant because many people around the world have grown up watching the Marvel movies and/or reading the comic books. Marvel has been a big influence in pop culture, and it continues to be today as they release new movies and shows.

Baby Groot

I built this sculpture of Baby Groot from Guardians of the Galaxy Vol. 2 because it was another way to represent Marvel. Moveover, the cultural significance of Baby Groot is about how his adorablness from the movie captured many people's attention.
